PHO (VIETNAMESE NOODLE SOUP)

Ingredients

  • 2kg of tubular bone
  • 1.2kg beef fillet
  • 0.7kg of beef corn
  • 1kg of noodle soup
  • 400g onions
  • Lemongrass 200g
  • Purple onion
  • Ginger
  • Herbs cook in broth: dried cinnamon, cardamom, tangerine peel, anise, aroma, coriander seeds
  • Served vegetables: spring onion, cilantro, cilantro, lemon, chili, basil, braised vegetables Spices for beef noodle soup: salt, fish sauce, seasoning seeds, sugar

Instruction

  1. Onion peeled, 1 part thinly sliced, 1 part sliced areca zone. Wash cilantro, finely chopped.The scallion, separate the head and the chopped leaves. The accompanying vegetables washed, drained.
  2. You put dried cinnamon, cardamom, tangerine peel, anise, cloves, cilantro seeds in a cloth bag, tie it tightly
  3. Washed cow bone. Put a pot of water on the stove with a little lemongrass to boil. Put the bones in a little blanch to eliminate bad odors. Take out the poached bones, put in a tray with ginger, purple onion, cut areca onions and bake until the bones are golden, remove the bones from the oven, immediately put in an ice bath.
  4. Onion peeled, 1 part thinly sliced, 1 part sliced areca zone. Wash cilantro, finely chopped. The scallion, separate the head and the chopped leaves. The accompanying vegetables washed, drained.
  5. Boil a pot of water, put grilled beef bones, seasoning package, grilled onions, ginger, lemongrass in a pot of water. Bone tunnel about 5 - 6 hours to produce fresh water. While cooking the bones, you add the beef to boil. Note that boiled beef, then picked out immediately if it is too long, the meat will be soft. Then take the ingredients out, filter to get the broth. Place a pot of filtered broth on the stove, season with a little salt, monosodium glutamate, seasoning seeds, rock sugar to taste, boil and turn off the heat.
